What Makes A Company Stable

Key markers shown on every card.

Profitability

Shows whether the business is making profit and whether profit is growing or falling.

Debt Level

Uses debt to equity and interest coverage to show whether borrowings look manageable.

Cash Flow

Positive operating cash flow is a strong sign the business converts sales into cash.

Return Ratios

ROE and ROCE help show how efficiently the company uses money to generate returns.

Promoter Quality

Promoter holding and pledge status can signal confidence and financial stress.

Volume Strength

Volume helps explain whether market interest is rising or whether liquidity is weak.

Build With AI

The lowest-maintenance way to turn this into a real product.

1. Design the first version with AI

Use Firebase Studio or v0 to generate the interface from a prompt. Keep the app static at first so there are no user logins, no backend, and almost no maintenance.

2. Precompute the stock data daily

Pull official filings and market data after market close, generate a JSON file, and publish the new cards once a day instead of on every visit.

3. Deploy it free

Host the static site on Cloudflare Pages or GitHub Pages. This is the cheapest and easiest path for a simple public website.
